About Watergardens
Watergardens is a small coding exercise for me, but also a memory to my past aquascapes.

Plant portrait images are almost all Bucephalandra, beautiful plants that grow endemically in Borneo. The leaves exhibit a metallic effect, and the plants can form submerged inflorescences.
